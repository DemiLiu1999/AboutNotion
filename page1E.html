Creating a New Page

Every new page that you create is a blank cavas. You can add whatever content you like, from texts to images, to-do lists, to powerful databases. All pages on your workspace live in the left-hand side bar.

To create a new page, click the new page button at the bottom left of your notion window.

You’ll see a menu of page types. You can click on them to turn your empty page into a table, or a board for instance.

You can enter your title here.

You can type in your content here.

To see your list of different types of content you can add to your page, just type slash. This will pull out the slash menu with all the available content types.

Finally, you can add a memorable icon to your page to make it easier to find later.
