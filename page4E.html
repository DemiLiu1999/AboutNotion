Template

To find our template collection, go to Templates in the side bar.

Or access templates here whenever you create a new page.

Our template picker is separated into sections that will help you navigate it better.

To add it into your work space, click use this template.

More advanced databases like this Class Notes database comes with instructions and best practices at the top.

You’ll notice that this database is full of content when viewing on the template picker. But once you add it to your own workspace, we automatically delete most of the placeholder content for you. Once the templates added to your workspace, you could start editing them to your fancy.

For instance, you can hide the instructions at the top and add a cover photo to your page.

To browse even more templates, select Full Template Gallery at the bottom right of the temple’s window. You’ll get to view and use all the templates.
