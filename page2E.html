Writing&Editing Basics

When you highlight any words, this menu will pop up.

The first one is text. You can turn your text into different blocks, like headings.

The second one is the Link which can turn any text into a hyper link by clicking on it.

The third one is comment. You can leave a comment on any selection of text. If these comments are addressed to the specific team members, you can mention their name like this.

Next is the Bold, Italics, strike-through, and code notation options.

Next is color. You can pick your preferred text color from this list or text background color.

The last one is the mention option. It allows you to connect your page to dates, people, and pages.
